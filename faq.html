Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Expense Manager?
Expense Manager is a tool designed to help you track and manage your expenses efficiently.
How do I add a new expense?
To add a new expense, click on the "Add Expense" button and fill out the required details.
Can I edit or delete an expense?
Yes, you can edit or delete an expense by clicking the corresponding buttons next to each expense entry.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
Can I export my expenses?
Yes, you can export your expenses in various formats such as CSV or PDF.
How do I create a budget?
To create a budget, navigate to the "Budgets" section and click on the "Create Budget" button.
What categories can I use for my expenses?
You can categorize your expenses into various categories like Food, Travel, Utilities, etc.
How do I set up recurring expenses?
To set up recurring expenses, go to the "Recurring Expenses" section and add a new recurring expense.
Can I access Expense Manager on my mobile device?
Yes, Expense Manager is mobile-friendly and can be accessed on any device with an internet connection.
How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the "Account Settings" section and click on "Reset Password".
Can I link my bank account to Expense Manager?
Yes, you can link your bank account for automatic transaction imports.
What support options are available?
We offer 24/7 support through email and live chat.
How do I customize my expense categories?
You can customize your expense categories in the "Settings" section.
Is there a limit to the number of expenses I can track?
No, there is no limit to the number of expenses you can track with Expense Manager.
Can I get detailed reports of my expenses?
Yes, you can generate detailed reports of your expenses in the "Reports" section.
